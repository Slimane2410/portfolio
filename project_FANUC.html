RETURN TO MATRIX

FANUC Robotic Casting Cell

Automated pouring cell for aluminum foundry operations, replacing manual crucible handling with a FANUC robot.

Mission Overview

The project defines and justifies a robotic cell to automate molten metal pouring in a foundry. The goal is to replace manual transport of a hot crucible with a controlled FANUC robot sequence to reduce cycle time, thermal losses, and human risk, while improving repeatability for aluminum casting.

Objective: design a safe, compliant, and productive cell (target < 60 s per cycle, estimated ~30 s with two parts per cycle), validated by simulation, risk analysis, and ROI evaluation.
