Data-Driven Reduced-Order Mechanical Model

Research-Oriented Engineering Project — Arts et Métiers ParisTech

Semester-long research project to develop a data-driven reduced-order model (Reduced Order Model) for a simple mechanical system, inspired by Professor Francisco Chinesta's work on model reduction and PGD. The goal is to build a full data science foundation within a clear mechanical framework by generating simulated data, performing analysis and preprocessing, applying dimensionality reduction (PCA), and building a predictive model that reproduces system dynamics. This work is designed to contribute to Francisco Chinesta's research directions within the lab.
